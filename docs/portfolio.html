C# Class Projects

Below are a few projects I have developed in C# while I have been at Grand Circus.

  • BlockBuster Simulation App:
    This allows a user to watch through a scenes of a movie that they can check out at a "BlockBuster". This project utilized more Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) than my earlier projects and it gave me a good understanding into the power it can provide. It includes both DVD and VHS selections and prompts user if they will rewind the VHS tapes. There is a fun feature where I used threading to simulate a scene at a time full movie experience, at least as much as you can inside a terminal window.

  • PigLatin Translator:
    This was an early app I developed. It is used to translate english into Pig Latin. It will ignore numbers and anything with a special character, while passing through puncuation.

  • Task List App:
    A look at my first project using OOP. An app used to log and assign tasks or "To-Dos" to people with due dates. It comes with preset tasks to display the functionality. In the future I plan on implementing searching or grouping by due date and adding people to assign to.

  • Password Validation:
    My first look into using Regex as a validation method to for emails, phone numbers and the date. Regex is a beast of a tool to use it is an eye sore. However, I know it has very valuable applications among validation.

More to Come! Including my own projects outside of class!
